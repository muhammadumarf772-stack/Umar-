7 Study Tips to Focus Better

Struggling to concentrate while studying? These simple study tips will help you stay focused, manage your time, and study more effectively.

1. Study in Short Sessions

Use the Pomodoro technique: study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break.

2. Remove Distractions

Keep your phone away and study in a quiet place to improve focus.

3. Make a Simple Study Plan

Planning reduces stress and helps you cover all subjects on time.

4. Use Active Learning

Write notes, teach someone else, or quiz yourself.

5. Stay Healthy

Drink water, sleep well, and eat balanced meals.

6. Revise Regularly

Revision strengthens memory and understanding.

7. Stay Consistent

Daily small effort is better than last-minute cramming.
